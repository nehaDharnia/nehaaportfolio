Minti aur Mitthu

I created a story and made comic book in which I have shown how minti and the character bird mitthu became friends

  • A girl named Minti used to live in a town in Amritsar. She was passionate about painting.
  • One day she went to nearby that station and saw a train there which had no colour on it. She thought of painting it. At that time the havaldar bablu saw her.
  • He was about to catch her when a bird came and pooped on his face. Seeing the opportunity Minti ran away from there.Minti asked the bird why it helped her, He told that earlier he used to live in the jungle. But one day while migrating he lost his way,he got separated from her friends and family.
  • She used to see Minti's paintings which reminded him of good times with his friends. minti got emotional on hearing this and then she asked the bird to be her friend, she also named bird mitthu.
